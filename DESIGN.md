# Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark editorial technical explainer. The frame should feel like a precise exhibit wall:
large institutional typography, measured rules, warm copper signals, muted sage
confirmation states, and deep ink surfaces with subtle paper grain. Motion is weighted,
directional, and calm enough to read at a glance.

## Colors

- Deep ink `#0A0F14`: canvas
- Ink lift `#111A22`: panels
- Bone `#F0EDE5`: primary text
- Copper `#E07A4F`: primary brand signal
- Sage `#87A88B`: secondary brand signal

## Typography

- Display/body: `Avenir Next` with system sans fallbacks
- Code/data: `IBM Plex Mono` when self-hosted, with `Menlo` fallback

## Motion

- Entrances use weighted ease-out curves and overlapping stagger.
- Data changes use tabular numerals and restrained spring settles.
- Ambient motion is slow, finite, and deterministic.
- Scene changes use directional wipes or focus transitions.

## What Not To Do

- No rainbow, cyan-on-purple, or gradient text.
- No identical card grids.
- No default web-dashboard chrome.
- No instant appearances or disappearances.
- No infinite animation loops.
